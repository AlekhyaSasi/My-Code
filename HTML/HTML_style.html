Few more elements examples - abbr, address, cite, bdo

The HTML abbr element defines an abbreviation or an acronym. Hover on the WHO to know its acronym

The WHO was founded in 1948.

HTML address element defines contact information (author/owner) of a document or an article. It is usually displayed in italic.

Written by John Doe.
Visit us at:
Example.com

The HTML cite element defines the title of a work. Browsers usually display it in italic.

The Scream by Edvard Munch. Painted in 1893.
